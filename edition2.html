WHY JAKE??!?!

In an ever-evolving age of “new,” smartphones and social media dependency have been on the rise. Social media’s original intention was to connect us but has ultimately separated us further from each other. This newsletter comes to you to lessen your dependency on this isolating technology. We aim to move away from these vices and support those choosing to use the “dumb phone.” Using email rather than social media, this newsletter will supply information on events as well as updates on the music scene within SLO. Without social media, you risk missing out on real social events, but this newsletter will allow you to skip the FOMO and stay in the loop of everything going on in the central coast scene.

 

This newsletter will update you about shows, gigs, concerts and other events. It will also feature concert reviews, artist spotlights, weekly playlists, and more.
 

Last weeks show at Linnea's

Last week, .wavzine put together a show for fire relief in L.A. In the back of Linnea's, out in their beautiful vine-covered patio, a crowd gathered to experience the sounds of two local bands: Amttrak and Donde.

Donde started the night off with funky grooves and high-energy vibes. Playing through the rain, they kept the energy up even while huddled under an umbrella. 

Amttrak came up next, drowning the crowd in intricate and contrasting guitar riffs, with soft vocals floating overhead and intricate drumming underneath it all. They were both loud and quiet, and unrelentingly great.

Upcoming Events

Bringing you multiple options for this weekend, we have shows at The Bunker, Dark Nectar, Frog and Peach, The Libertine, and  a Satellite of Love!

If you're looking for some high energy, heavy music, Dark Nectar has you covered! Bringing together Metal, Punk, and Hardcore shows, this venue is a small coffee shop in Atascadero covered in art work guaranteed to give you a loud, intimate show. 

Looking for something more chill? Frog and Peach are bringing Indie rock bands including Big Sierra and Skipping Breakfast. 

For a bigger stage and cheap prices, Libertine is offering fun 21+ shows consisting of Toad, The Plums and the Solar Garlic Experience. On top of this, every Tuesday you can enjoy an open mic comedy night. 

Rock music not quite your thing? We got you covered! Head on over to The Bunker to enjoy a night of Jazz and art in this art gallery. All of the proceeds from this event will also support music educators affected by the Eaton fire. 

To help continue the support for those who are struggling from the fires in LA, head on over to Satellite of Love this Saturday!
